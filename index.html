History

The events that we have conducted so far!

Melam '21

( Onam Celebration )

Melam was the first event that E18eens organised. Melam was held on August 25th, 2022. Melam is a onam celebration, which is a harvest festival held in the college. The college was festooned with flowers, and students had designed a pookalam in front of the administrative block.Various games such as tug of war, sack jump, musical chairs, and other entertaining activities were played at the event.And then there was the onam sadya, which was served to everyone.

Logo Reveal

A logo is the pehechan, and the E18eens have one as well. On November 18, 2021, the logo of the E18eens was unveiled in front of a large crowd in the administrative block. Later, a nasik band performed outside the campus, which all of the students loved and it has brighten the day.

Vanakkam '22

( Fresher's Day )

Freshmen day marks the beginning of a new chapter in one's life.E18eens held one of the nicest freshmen parties ever seen. The newcomers had a great time at the events.The festivities were a big hit with the newbies. Everyone had a great time because it was the first event for the second and third years.

Jashn E Rang

( Holi Celebration )

On March 22, 2022, E18eens had celebrated Jashn e rang, a festival of colours. It included a variety of games as well as other entertaining activities. This added another incident to the E18eens' memory.

PAPL '22 Winners

Chakkara Pandhal

Due to covid, the eighteens batch was unable to conduct the PAPL...however, when the Nineteens batch conducted the PAPL, the eighteens batch teams, chakkarapandal fc and final strikers, reached the semi final...and the chakkarapandhal fc were crowned THE CHAMPIONS.
